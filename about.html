about us

{about san antonio coding challenge}

Starting in January 2016, the San Antonio Coding Challenge is a monthly networking event featuring a coding competition and other fun games. The event is put together by volunteers from the San Antonio Coding Challenge Meetup group (previously known as The San Antonio PHP Meetup group) and paid by the generous donations of our sponsors. The monthly event Coding Challenge is free and open for everyone.

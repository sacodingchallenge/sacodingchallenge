challenge de mayo - May 26, 2017

description

There will be 3-5 fun challenges (of varying complexity) with points awarded for the completion of each one. Highest score wins. Prizes will be awarded to 1st, 2nd and 3rd place winners. There will be 2 divisions - Students and Professionals.
This month, our food theme will be based on Cinco De Mayo. We plan on doing the grilling out on the street!
Doors open at 6:30 for registration. The coding challenge will then begin at 7:00 and will take about an hour. Prizes are awarded at 8:15. You do not have to participate in the challenge to attend.
Here is the schedule -
6:30 - 6:45 - Registration
6:45 - 7:00 - Human Bingo
7:00 - 7:30 - Movie Trivia Contest
7:00 - 8:00 - Coding Challenge
7:30 - 8:00- Cup Stacking Challenge
8:00- 8:15 - Raffle with fun prizes
8:15 - Awards presentation
